Meet the Team

we powerpuff corp, a team of four, based in hawthorn. we a game development company with a passion for building creative and engaging experiences. with four dedicated members, we bring together different skills and ideas to design and develop. Each of us focuses on different areas, but together we create games and digital content that are fun, innovative, and inspiring. When we put it all together, it feels like unlocking the final boss: teamwork. Our team is growing, and we're excited to welcome anyone who wants to code, design, or just bring wild ideas to the table. Join us if you like building games, sharing ideas, and maybe debating which game soundtrack is the best (spoiler: we don't always agree).
Curious? Excited? Think you'd enjoy being part of our team? Check our Jobs and Apply pages to see how you can hop on board!
